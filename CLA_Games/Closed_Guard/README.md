# Closed Guard Games


## Game 1

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get up to both feet and open the guard

Bottom Player: Get inside the elbows and connect chest-to-chest or clear the hands and stay outside the elbows

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. No sweeps, submissions, or transitions

Starting Position: Start in Closed Guard position with Top Player hands on Bottom Player. Bottom Player has no grips


## Game 2

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get up to both feet and open the guard OR force half guard

Bottom Player: Force hips to the mat or get outside the elbows

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. No sweeps or submissions

Starting Position: Bottom player starts with inside position, hands connected, chest-to-chest


## Game 3

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get square with both hands on partner

Bottom Player: Become the top player

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds

Starting Position: Bottom player starts outside the elbows


## Game 4

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get square with both hands on partner

Bottom Player: Become the top player or attach hips to shoulders

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds

Starting Position: Bottom player starts outside the elbows


## Game 5

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get hands back to safety OR force half guard

Bottom Player: Become the top player or attach hips to shoulders

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds

Starting Position: Bottom player starts with an Overhook and collar tie.


## Game 6

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Preserve a strong and safe posture

Bottom Player: Get partner's hands to the mat

Constraints: There is no winning condition. Surviving under pressure/threat is key

Starting Position: Closed Guard position with no grips by any player


## Game 7

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Escape (open the guard or force half guard)

Bottom Player: Get hands to the mat and lock legs around head and arm

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. No sweeps or submissions

Starting Position: Closed guard with no grips for either player


## Game 8

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get both arms to the inside/outside of the legs

Bottom Player: Keep position for as long as possible

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. No sweeps, no submissions, no transitions

Starting Position: Top Player starts with one arm in, one arm out of Bottom Player's legs (i.e. Triangle Position)


## Game 9

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Stay safe and square with the Bottom Player

Bottom Player: Lock the Triangle Choke

Constraints: Top Player has no winning condition. No Sweeps or transitions

Starting Position: Start in Triangle/Diamond Guard position


## Game 10

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Get both hands to the inside/outside.

Bottom Player: Get a submission or become the Top Player

Constraints: Top Player must maintain winning position for 3 seconds.

Starting Position: Start in Triangle/Diamond Guard position


## Game 11

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Open the guard or force the Half Guard

Bottom Player: Lock hips around head and shoulders

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. No sweeps, submissions, or transitions for bottom player

Starting Position: Start in Closed Guard position with Top Player posting on Bottom Player's body. Bottom Player has no grips


## Game 12

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Work towards general goals from previous games

Bottom Player: Work towards general goals from previous games

Constraints: Must maintain winning position for 3 seconds

Starting Position: Bottom Player starts with grips that change each round.


## Game 13

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Open the guard or force half guard

Bottom Player: Keep Top Player in Closed Guard and on knees

Constraints: Top Player must maintain winning position for 3 seconds. Bottom Player has no winning condition. No sweeps, no submissions, no transitions for Bottom Player.

Starting Position: Start in standard Closed Guard position with neither player having grips.


## Game 14

### <strong>Objectives</strong>
<br>
Top Player: Break the collar grip

Bottom Player: Connect the free hand to the Top Player's neck

Constraints: No sweeps or transitions

Starting Position: Bottom Player starts with a cross collar grip
